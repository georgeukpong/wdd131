FashionNaija Site Plan

Site Name

FashionNaija.com

FashionNaija.com was chosen to reflect the trendy, stylish, and culturally rich fashion scene in Nigeria. “Naija” is a popular and affectionate nickname for Nigeria, making the name both recognizable and locally relevant. It speaks to a youthful and modern audience while celebrating Nigerian heritage.

Logo

Below is a placeholder for the site logo. The final logo will use green and white (the colors of the Nigerian flag) and incorporate a bold modern typeface to represent elegance and confidence.

Target Audience

FashionNaija is designed for young adults, style influencers, and fashion-forward individuals in Nigeria, especially those in urban areas like Abuja and Lagos. Our audience values creativity, cultural pride, and staying on top of modern fashion trends.

Site Purpose

The purpose of FashionNaija.com is to showcase and sell modern Nigerian fashion pieces that combine traditional designs with contemporary styles. The site will also provide inspiration, style tips, and customer reviews to help visitors make confident fashion choices. Whether you’re shopping for a special event or just want to upgrade your wardrobe, FashionNaija offers a seamless online experience.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: Where can I find high-quality, modern Nigerian outfits for an upcoming wedding?
  • Scenario 2: Does this shop offer traditional fabric options like Ankara or Aso Oke with custom tailoring?

Color Scheme

  • Green: #008753 – Primary color for headings, buttons, and accents.
  • White: #FFFFFF – Background and clean spaces for readability.
  • Black: #000000 – Text color for strong contrast and elegance.

Typography

The site will use a combination of elegant and modern fonts to match its stylish aesthetic. Playfair Display is used for the main headings and content in the site plan document to reflect sophistication and fashion-forward branding. For the final website, headings may also use Montserrat or Poppins for a cleaner, minimalist look, while body text will use highly readable fonts like Open Sans or Roboto. This combination ensures both beauty and usability across all screen sizes.
